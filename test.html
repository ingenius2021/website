About Us

inGenius 2020 is an annual 24-hour hackathon organised by PES University, Electronic City Campus. With over 3500 registrations over the past eight years, we're back again.

Circumstances may have forced us online, but that came as a blessing in disguise. Because this time, we've upped the stakes as we're going fully global That's right, inGenius is open to registrations to each and any one of you that wants to take part, wherever you are.

No restrictions, no criteria, and NO FEE.

FAQs

Can I attend inGenius 2020?

inGenius is open to all college students from any year, branch or degree across the globe

Is there a registration fee?

No, there is no registration fee

Whats the preferred team size?

Only team size of 2-4 members will be considered for judging and be eligible for prizes

Can we participate individually?

Yes, you can but only teams of 2-4 people will be eligible for prizes and judging.

Where is the venue?

There is no venue. Due to the pandemic we are conducting the event virtually.

What is the theme of inGenius 2020?

It's an open hack and all domains are allowed

What should I submit at the end of the hackathon?

You will have to show a working proof of concept of your idea.

Where can I submit the working proof?

We are using the Devfolio as our platform to conduct the hackathon and hence submissions will be on the same.

How will you judge?

The panel of judges will consist of industry experts and professionals. They will go through the executable file and will have an interview round with top 10 teams to decide on final evaluations.

I have a question that's not answered here

We're here to help you however you need! Feel free to drop us an email. You could also reach out to us on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter or Linkedin
